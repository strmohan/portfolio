Project-detail

  • User Registration and Authentication: Users can create accounts, log in, and manage their profiles.
    Password encryption and secure authentication mechanisms should be implemented.
  • Product Catalog: Display a wide range of products with details such as name, description, price and images
    Implement categories and subcategories for easy navigation and filtering of products
  • Shopping Cart: Users can add products to their cart and view the cart contents. Enable users to modify quantities, remove items, and calculate the total price.
  • Order Management: Users can place orders Admin panel for managing orders, updating their status, and generating reports.
  • Responsive Design:Ensure the website is fully responsive and works well on various devices (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Admin Panel: A separate admin panel to manage products, categories, us accounts, and orders. Provide role-based access control to restrict certain actions to admin users only
